As people rely on social media for their daily communication, cyberbully also occur from the freedom and chaos of Internet.
A research about people behaviour when online is on going.

Scope:

Methodology:

Observe different platforms to identify how people behave when certain scenarios happen.

Observation:

The experiment is taken by observing 5 posts with a same topic on 3 social media application.
Application been observe is Instagram, Facebook and RED.
