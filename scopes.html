Scope Of Study

  • How do people behave online? Polite or aggressive?
  • With the same topic, which platform can gain more interest for the viewers?
  • Which social media is least famous or most famous, and which app did users commonly prefer to use?
  • How long does it take for friends to find our new account?

Methodology

  • Observe different platforms to identify how people behave when certain scenarios happen.
  • Create multiple accounts at different platforms with the same topic and observe which one can gather more followers, for one month.
  • Do a survey using google form to collect feedback from users.
  • Create a new account at Instagram, and record the result every week for 1 month.
